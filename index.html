About Me

Hey there! Meet Me! A versatile and passionate software engineer with a unique blend of business and information technology education. My academic journey started with a focus on understanding the intersection of technology and business, earning a degree in Business and Information Technology from university. Eager to delve deeper into the world of software development, I pursued additional certifications at Moringa School in full-stack web development and later at ALX Africa in software engineering, honing my technical skills and staying abreast of the latest industry trends.

Throughout my career, I have applied my knowledge through freelance work, designing standalone websites and developing full-stack web applications.
